Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Noman Haroon ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use The Calories Tracker App.

Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: age, weight, height, gender, and calorie goals
  • Food Logs: meals, calories, nutrition data, and notes
  • Images: food images uploaded for AI scanning
  • Usage Data: app usage, interactions, and crash reports
  • Device Info: device type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide calorie tracking and meal logging
  • Analyze food images using AI
  • Sync data across devices
  • Improve app performance

AI Processing

Food images may be sent to AI providers such as OpenAI to estimate calories and nutrition. These results are estimates and may not always be accurate.

Data Storage

Your data may be stored securely using cloud services such as Firebase to allow syncing across devices.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.
